About Pathways to Policy

Our mission is to democratize access to policy careers and create pathways for diverse voices in governance.

Pathways to Policy was founded on the belief that effective policy-making requires diverse perspectives and inclusive participation. We recognize that traditional pathways to policy careers often exclude talented individuals from underrepresented communities.

Our Mission

We work to break down barriers and create accessible entry points for individuals passionate about public service and policy change. Through our platform, we connect aspiring policy professionals with organizations, resources, and fellowship opportunities that can launch and accelerate their careers.

What We Do

Our comprehensive platform serves as a bridge between talent and opportunity. We curate and organize policy-related organizations, educational resources, and fellowship programs to help individuals navigate the complex landscape of policy careers.

Whether you're a recent graduate, career changer, or experienced professional looking to pivot into policy work, Pathways to Policy provides the tools and connections you need to succeed.
