About Pathways to Policy

I built Pathways to Policy to democratize access to policy careers and create a better policy talent pipeline.

Pathways to Policy was founded because I realized that policy opportunities were hard to find and very opaque to outsiders and wanted to help fix this.

What We Do

Pathways to Policy compiles a whole host of policy organizations, resources, and opportunities in one place, breaking down barriers and making policy opportunities accessible to everyone.
